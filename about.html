About Candor

Built for the people doing the work.

Candor is a candidate-first professional platform built on a simple belief — the people doing the work should hold the power in the hiring process.

Our Mission

To give every professional the power, the information, and the dignity they deserve in their career.

Why We Exist

The system is broken. Everyone knows it.

Salaries hidden until the final round. Candidates ghosted with no explanation. Names judged before work ever gets a chance. Offers rescinded after weeks of process. Every major job platform was designed to serve companies.

We built Candor because we lived it. And because the people applying for jobs deserve better than the industry they are applying into.

What We Do

Companies apply to you. Not the other way around.

You build a profile based on your work and your outcomes — not your CV. You set your salary floor, your terms, and your deal-breakers. Companies pitch you with the real salary on the first message.

You stay anonymous until you choose otherwise. You decide who is worthy of your time. The job comes to you.

Why We Are Different

Structure changes everything.

Every other platform makes money from companies. That means every other platform is built for companies. Candor makes money when professionals win. That is not a positioning statement. It is a structural decision that changes everything about how the platform works.

Salary transparency enforced on every pitch. Real number on the first message. Always. No exceptions.

Ghosting has consequences. Ghost a candidate and you lose platform access. Permanently.

Your identity stays hidden until you say so. Your boss will never know you are here.

We never sell your data to the people trying to hire you.

Our Values

What we stand for.

Honesty above comfort

We say the things the industry does not want said. We publish the data others hide.

Transparency above convenience

Salary visible. Culture verified. Hiring manager named. Always.

The professional above the platform

Every product decision starts with one question — does this serve the candidate?

Accountability above excuses

Bad actors lose access. No warnings. No second chances. The standard is the standard.
