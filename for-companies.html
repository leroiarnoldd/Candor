The shift

Why hiring works differently on Candor.

Candor was built because the hiring industry has been broken for decades. Companies spend £thousands per hire — on agency fees, recruiter tools, and time-consuming processes — and still hire the wrong people. Candidates ghost. Offers get declined late. Culture mismatches become expensive exits.

The root cause is simple. The incentives are misaligned. Job boards are paid to show candidates jobs. Recruitment agencies are paid per placement, regardless of fit. Recruiter platforms are paid to give you more names, not better ones. Nobody is paid for the quality of the match.

On Candor, you pay to pitch. Candidates earn to engage. Everyone is incentivised to make good matches.

That single mechanical shift changes everything downstream. Pitches become more considered. Candidates respond more honestly. Hiring becomes about fit again — not volume.

Real talk

The honest bits.

Candor does things differently. That means some companies will not like what we are doing. Here are the most common objections — and our honest answers.

"Salary on the first message? That kills our negotiation leverage."
It does — and that is the point. The entire market benefits from salary transparency. If your salary is genuinely competitive, transparency works in your favour. If it is not, the problem is not transparency. It is the salary. Candidates on Candor still negotiate — on equity, flexibility, scope, bonus. The base is just a starting point, not a game.
"What happens if we accidentally miss a candidate message?"
The first time, you get a warning. You will have 7 days to respond before it becomes an incident. Three incidents in 6 months results in a formal warning published on your profile. Repeated failure results in platform removal. Ghosting is not punished by mistake — it is only punished when it becomes a pattern.
"Can we still use our existing ATS?"
Yes. Candor integrates with the major ATS platforms on our Enterprise tier — Greenhouse, Workable, Lever, BambooHR, and more. Pitches sent through Candor can flow directly into your pipeline so your hiring managers work in the tools they already know.
"How do we know candidates are real?"
Every candidate on Candor is verified. We check email, work history, and core skills before a profile is made visible. For premium candidates, we verify through demonstration — real task-based assessments reviewed by practising experts. You are not getting CV claims. You are getting evidence.
"£2,000 a month for unlimited pitches seems low. What is the catch?"
No catch. Candor is priced deliberately below industry norms because we believe hiring infrastructure should not be a luxury product. The revenue model works because companies pay per pitch and candidates engage more genuinely — so you need fewer pitches to make a hire.
"Are agencies really banned?"
Yes. Only internal hiring managers, HR leaders, and verified recruiters working directly for a named company can send pitches on Candor. Third-party recruitment agencies, independent headhunters, and staffing firms are prohibited. This is one of the platform's core rules and it is not negotiable.
